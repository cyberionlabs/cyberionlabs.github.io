Our Services

Application Pentesting

Our application penetration testing services encompass the identification, validation, and prioritization of security vulnerabilities across web applications, mobile applications, thick client applications, and virtual applications.

Source Code Review

Detect application security vulnerabilities at an earlier stage in your software development lifecycle, specifically during the source code level.

Threat Modeling

Our Threat Modeling service adopts a comprehensive strategy to recognize potential threats to your organization's systems and applications. It delivers actionable insights that empower decision-makers with prioritized vulnerabilities, outlined attack scenarios, and personalized remediation suggestions, facilitating informed strategic choices.

Network Pentesting

Our network penetration testing offerings encompass the identification, validation, and prioritization of vulnerabilities within internal, externally accessible, and cloud-based IT infrastructure.

Red Team Operations

Conducting simulated attacks via a red team engagement elevates the effectiveness of your information security program. Our red team exercises rigorously evaluate your organization's security controls, policies, incident response capabilities, and cybersecurity training initiatives.

Attack Surface Management

Enhance your asset and exposure management with our Attack Surface Management (ASM) solutions. Benefit from round-the-clock monitoring through continuous penetration testing and leverage our expert team, advanced technology, and thorough methodology to proactively identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them.
